# Banking Support Knowledge Base

## 1. Account Management

### Opening a New Account
- Eligible account types: Savings, Current, Fixed Deposit, Recurring Deposit
- Required documents: Government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar/Passport/Driving License), PAN card, address proof, passport-size photograph
- Minimum opening balance: Savings – ₹1,000 | Current – ₹10,000
- Apply online via the bank's website or visit the nearest branch
- Account activation takes 2–3 business days after document verification

### Updating Personal Information
- Name change: submit a written request with legal name-change document at the branch
- Address change: update via **NetBanking > Profile > Update Address** or visit branch with new address proof
- Mobile number update: visit branch with ID proof; OTP-based update available for minor changes via app
- Email update: log in to NetBanking > Profile > Contact Details

### Account Closure
- Submit an account closure form at the branch
- Ensure zero balance before closure (or transfer remaining balance)
- Outstanding loans, standing instructions, or linked investments must be settled first
- Closure processing takes 7 business days; confirmation sent via registered email

## 2. Transactions & Transfers

### Fund Transfer Methods
| Method | Limit Per Transaction | Availability | Charges |
|--------|----------------------|--------------|---------|
| IMPS | ₹5,00,000 | 24x7 | ₹5 (above ₹1,000) |
| NEFT | ₹10,00,000 | Mon–Sat, 8 AM–7 PM | ₹2.50–₹25 |
| RTGS | ₹2,00,000 minimum | Mon–Sat, 9 AM–4:30 PM | ₹25–₹50 |
| UPI | ₹1,00,000 | 24x7 | Free |

### Failed Transaction
- If deducted but not credited: wait 2 business days for auto-reversal
- If not reversed, raise a dispute via **NetBanking > Raise Dispute > Failed Transaction**
- Attach transaction reference number and screenshot
- Investigation takes up to 7 business days; refund credited to source account
- For UPI failures, contact your UPI app's support with the UPI transaction ID

### Unauthorized Transaction
1. Immediately block your card/account via the bank's helpline or app
2. Report the unauthorized transaction within 3 business days
3. File a complaint via **NetBanking > Complaints > Unauthorized Transaction**
4. The bank will investigate; provisional credit may be given within 10 working days
5. Also file a police complaint (cybercrime) and share the FIR with the bank

## 3. Cards (Debit & Credit)

### Card Blocked or Not Working
- **ATM decline**: Check daily withdrawal limit; default is ₹25,000/day
- **POS decline**: Ensure contactless/international/online transactions are enabled in app
- **Card blocked**: Unblock via **Banking App > Cards > Manage Card > Unblock**
- **Suspected fraud block**: Call the 24x7 helpline; re-verification required

### Card Lost or Stolen
1. Immediately block the card: **App > Cards > Block Card** or call helpline
2. File a police report if stolen
3. Request a replacement card: **App > Cards > Request Replacement**
4. Replacement delivery: 5–7 business days to registered address
5. Virtual card can be issued instantly for online transactions while waiting

### Credit Card Bill Payment
- Pay via NetBanking, UPI, NEFT, or auto-debit mandate
- Minimum payment: 5% of outstanding balance or ₹200, whichever is higher
- Due date is printed on the statement; late payment fee: ₹500–₹1,000
- Full payment avoids interest; partial payment attracts 3.5% per month on outstanding

## 4. Loans

### Home Loan Application
- Eligibility: Age 21–65, minimum income ₹25,000/month, CIBIL score ≥ 700
- Required documents: income proof (salary slips/ITR), property documents, ID and address proof
- Processing time: 15–30 business days from complete document submission
- Loan amount: up to 80% of property value
- Current interest rate: 8.5%–9.5% p.a. (floating, linked to repo rate)

### Loan EMI Issues
- **Missed EMI**: A penalty of 2% per month on the overdue amount is levied
- **EMI bounce**: Ensure sufficient balance in the linked account 2 days before due date
- **Prepayment**: Floating rate loans — no prepayment penalty; fixed rate — 2% of prepaid amount
- **EMI date change**: Submit a written request at branch; allowed once per loan tenure

## 5. NetBanking & Mobile App

### Login Issues
- Forgot password: click **Forgot Password** on login page, verify via OTP on registered mobile
- Account locked (5 failed attempts): call helpline or visit branch with ID proof for unlock
- NetBanking not activated: register via ATM or branch with account number and debit card

### Transaction Limit Settings
- Modify daily transfer limits under **NetBanking > Settings > Transaction Limits**
- For limit increases beyond default, OTP + debit card verification required
- International transaction enable/disable: **App > Cards > International Transactions**

### App Not Loading / Crashing
1. Update the app to the latest version from Play Store / App Store
2. Clear app cache: Settings > Apps > Bank App > Clear Cache
3. Ensure your device OS is updated (app requires Android 8+ or iOS 13+)
4. Uninstall and reinstall if the issue persists
5. Check if the bank has announced scheduled maintenance on its website/social media
