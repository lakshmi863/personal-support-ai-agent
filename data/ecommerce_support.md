# E-commerce Support Knowledge Base

## 1. Orders & Tracking

### How to Place an Order
1. Browse products and click **Add to Cart**
2. Go to **Cart > Proceed to Checkout**
3. Enter your shipping address and select a delivery method
4. Choose a payment method and click **Place Order**
5. A confirmation email with your order ID will be sent within 5 minutes

### Tracking Your Order
- Log in and go to **My Orders**
- Click on the order to view real-time tracking status
- A tracking number is emailed once the order is shipped (usually within 24 hours)
- Use the carrier's website with the tracking number for detailed location updates

### Order Status Definitions
| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Pending | Order received, payment being verified |
| Confirmed | Payment successful, preparing for dispatch |
| Shipped | Package handed to carrier |
| Out for Delivery | Package with local delivery agent |
| Delivered | Package delivered to address |
| Cancelled | Order cancelled before dispatch |

## 2. Returns & Refunds

### Return Policy
- Items can be returned within **30 days** of delivery
- Products must be unused, in original packaging with tags intact
- Non-returnable items: perishables, digital downloads, personal hygiene products
- To initiate a return: go to **My Orders > Select Order > Request Return**

### Refund Policy
- Refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** after the returned item is received
- Refund method: original payment method (credit card, UPI, wallet)
- Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
- You will receive a refund confirmation email once processed

### Damaged or Wrong Item Received
- Report within **48 hours** of delivery via **Help > Report an Issue**
- Attach photos of the damaged/wrong item
- A replacement will be dispatched or a full refund issued within 3 business days
- Do not discard packaging until the issue is resolved

## 3. Payments

### Accepted Payment Methods
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
- Net Banking
- EMI (available on orders above ₹3,000 on select cards)
- Cash on Delivery (available in select pin codes)
- Store Wallet / Gift Cards

### Payment Failure Troubleshooting
1. Ensure card details (number, expiry, CVV) are entered correctly
2. Check if your bank has blocked the transaction — call your bank's helpline
3. Try a different payment method
4. Clear browser cache or try a different browser
5. If amount was debited but order not placed, it will be auto-refunded within 5–7 business days

### EMI Options
- Available on credit cards from HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak
- EMI tenure: 3, 6, 9, 12 months
- No-cost EMI available on select products (processing fee may apply)
- EMI details shown at checkout based on your card's eligibility

## 4. Shipping & Delivery

### Delivery Timeframes
| Delivery Type | Estimated Time |
|---------------|----------------|
| Standard | 5–7 business days |
| Express | 2–3 business days |
| Same Day | Available in select cities, order before 12 PM |
| International | 10–15 business days |

### Shipping Charges
- Free shipping on orders above ₹499
- Standard shipping: ₹49
- Express shipping: ₹99
- International shipping: calculated at checkout based on weight and destination

### Undelivered Package
- If marked delivered but not received:
  1. Check with neighbors or building security
  2. Wait 24 hours (sometimes delivery is marked early)
  3. Contact support with your order ID if still not received
  4. Investigation takes 2–3 business days; replacement or refund issued accordingly

## 5. Account Management

### Updating Delivery Address
- Go to **My Account > Addresses > Add/Edit Address**
- You can save multiple addresses and set a default
- Address changes apply to future orders only; existing orders cannot be redirected

### Cancelling an Order
- Orders can be cancelled before they are marked **Shipped**
- Go to **My Orders > Select Order > Cancel Order**
- Refund for prepaid orders processed within 5–7 business days
- COD orders: no refund needed; order simply won't be delivered
