# Telecom Support Knowledge Base

## 1. Mobile Plans & Recharge

### Prepaid Recharge
- Recharge via: official app, website, authorized retailers, UPI apps (Paytm, PhonePe)
- Recharge takes effect within 5 minutes; if delayed beyond 30 minutes, contact support
- If recharge debited but balance not credited, share transaction ID with support for reversal within 24 hours
- Auto-recharge: set up via **App > Recharge > Auto-Recharge** to avoid service interruption

### Postpaid Bill Payment
- Bill generated on the same date each month (as per billing cycle)
- Payment methods: NetBanking, UPI, credit/debit card, auto-pay mandate
- Due date: 15 days after bill generation; late payment surcharge: ₹100 or 2% of outstanding
- Itemized bill available under **My Account > Bills > Download**
- Dispute a charge: **My Account > Bills > Raise Dispute** within 60 days of bill date

### Plan Change & Add-ons
- Change prepaid pack: **App > Plans > Browse & Activate** (takes effect from next recharge)
- Postpaid plan change: effective from next billing cycle
- Add-ons (extra data, roaming, OTT): activate instantly via app
- International roaming packs must be activated before travel; standard rates apply without a pack

## 2. Network & Connectivity Issues

### No Network / Signal
1. Toggle Airplane mode ON for 10 seconds, then OFF
2. Restart your device
3. Check if there is a network outage in your area: **App > Network > Check Outage**
4. Ensure correct network mode is selected: Settings > Mobile Network > Preferred Network (4G/5G)
5. Re-insert SIM card (power off device first)
6. If issue persists beyond 2 hours, contact support with your location

### Slow Internet Speed
- Check your daily data balance: **App > My Usage > Data Balance**
- After fair usage policy (FUP) limit is reached, speeds reduce to 64 Kbps
- Run a speed test at fast.com and note down results before contacting support
- Disable background app refresh to improve effective speed
- In congested areas, switch between 4G and 5G manually if available
- Reset APN settings: Settings > Mobile Network > APN > Reset to Default

### 5G Not Available
- 5G requires a 5G-compatible device and SIM
- Check 5G coverage map at coverage.example.com
- Ensure 5G is enabled: Settings > Mobile Network > 5G On
- Upgrade SIM to 5G-enabled SIM at any store (free replacement)

## 3. SIM Card Services

### New SIM Activation
- Activation takes up to 4 hours after purchase and document submission
- Required: Aadhaar-based eKYC or in-store biometric verification
- Port-in SIM: number porting takes up to 7 days; service continues on old network until porting completes

### SIM Replacement (Lost/Damaged)
1. Block the SIM immediately: call 24x7 helpline or use the app
2. Visit the nearest store with original ID proof
3. New SIM issued on the spot; activation within 2–4 hours
4. All contacts, data, and plan carry over to the new SIM

### Number Portability (MNP)
- Send SMS: PORT <your number> to 1900 from the number you wish to port
- You will receive a Unique Porting Code (UPC) valid for 15 days
- Submit UPC to the new operator store along with ID proof
- Porting completes in 3–7 working days; expect a 2-hour service interruption during switch

## 4. Broadband / Home Internet

### Router Setup
1. Connect the router to the ONT/modem using the provided ethernet cable
2. Power on the router and wait for all LEDs to stabilize (2–3 minutes)
3. Connect to the default Wi-Fi name (printed on router label)
4. Open a browser and navigate to 192.168.1.1 to complete setup
5. Change the default Wi-Fi password on first login

### Broadband Not Working
1. Check all physical cable connections (ONT to router, power cables)
2. Restart ONT and router (unplug for 30 seconds)
3. Check if the WAN/Internet LED on the router is solid (green = connected, red/blinking = issue)
4. Log in to router admin (192.168.1.1) and check WAN status
5. Contact support if WAN shows "Disconnected" — may indicate a line fault or account issue

### Wi-Fi Speed Issues
- Position the router centrally and away from walls and interference sources (microwave, cordless phones)
- Use 5 GHz band for faster speeds (shorter range) and 2.4 GHz for wider coverage
- Check connected devices count: **Router Admin > Connected Devices**
- Schedule a bandwidth test by calling support if speeds are consistently below plan speed

## 5. Billing & Account

### Understanding Your Telecom Bill
- Rental charges: monthly plan fee
- Usage charges: calls/SMS beyond plan limits, roaming charges
- Taxes: GST at 18% on all telecom services
- OTT subscription charges (if bundled plan includes Netflix, Amazon Prime, etc.)

### Disputed Charges
- International roaming charges: verify by cross-checking travel dates and usage logs
- Premium SMS charges: often caused by accidental subscriptions; unsubscribe by sending STOP to short code
- To block all premium content charges: send SMS **STOP** to **1909**
- Raise billing dispute within 60 days of the bill date via **App > Help > Billing Dispute**

### Disconnection & Reconnection
- Prepaid: service suspends after validity expiry; recharge to restore
- Postpaid: service suspended after 45 days of non-payment; reconnection fee ₹250 after settlement
- Permanent disconnection: submit written request at store; number released after 90 days
