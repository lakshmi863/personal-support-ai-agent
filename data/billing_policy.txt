# Billing Policy

## Billing Dispute Resolution Timeline
All billing disputes are reviewed within 3-5 business days.
Complex disputes involving multiple charges may take up to 10 business days.
You will receive an email confirmation once your dispute is resolved.

## How to Submit a Dispute
1. Log in to your account dashboard.
2. Navigate to Billing > Dispute a Charge.
3. Select the charge and provide a reason.
4. Submit the form and save your ticket number.

## Refund Processing
Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
Refunds appear on your original payment method.
