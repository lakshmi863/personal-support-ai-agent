# Slack Integration for Adsparkx
Stay updated by connecting your workspace to a Slack channel.
- Step 1: In the Adsparkx dashboard, go to Settings > Integrations.
- Step 2: Click the 'Connect Slack' button.
- Step 3: Choose the destination channel for alerts.
- Step 4: Authorize the app permissions.
Currently, notifications support: New User Registration, Critical Error Logs, and Daily Usage Reports.
