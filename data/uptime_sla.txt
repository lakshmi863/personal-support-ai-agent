# Uptime SLA Policy

## Service Level Agreement
We guarantee 99.9% uptime monthly.
Scheduled maintenance windows are announced 48 hours in advance.

## Downtime Resolution Timeline
Critical outages are resolved within 1 hour.
Major incidents are resolved within 4 hours.
Minor issues are resolved within 24 hours.

## Operational Impact
During downtime, all billing cycles are paused automatically.
Credits are issued for any downtime exceeding SLA guarantees.
