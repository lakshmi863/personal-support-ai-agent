# Setting Up Custom Domains
White-label your workspace by using a custom domain.
1. Create a CNAME record in your DNS provider (Cloudflare, GoDaddy).
2. Point the CNAME to 'proxy.adsparkx.com'.
3. In your dashboard, navigate to Settings > White-Label.
4. Enter your domain (e.g., support.mycompany.com) and click Verify.
SSL certificates are automatically generated via Let's Encrypt.
